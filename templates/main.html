LMU GROWL is designed to help students determine how much they should spend per day with the remaining dollars on their LION meal plan. It is not associated with Loyola Marymount University. This web-app is currently functional only for the Fall 2017 semester, and assumes that students will be on campus until the last day of finals (December 15th, 2017).
Created by Maddie Louis and Leigh Lewis.
